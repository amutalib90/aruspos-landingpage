# ArusPay Merchant Operating System (Merchant OS)

## Strategic Product Vision & Roadmap

**Prepared for:** CEO\
**Prepared by:** Product Strategy

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Summary

ArusPay should not compete as just another Payment Gateway or POS
vendor.

Our long-term objective is to become the **Merchant Operating System
(Merchant OS)** for SMEs.

The strategy is simple:

> **Free Merchant Tools → Daily Merchant Usage → Higher Transaction
> Volume → Payment Gateway Revenue → Financial Services Expansion**

Instead of selling software licenses, ArusPay creates an ecosystem where
merchants run their daily business, while every payment flows through
ArusPay.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Vision

> **To become the operating system that powers every Malaysian SME.**

Success is not measured by how many POS systems we sell.

Success is measured by:

-   Active merchants
-   Daily active usage
-   Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)
-   Payment transactions processed
-   Merchant retention
-   Revenue per merchant

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Core Problem

Most SMEs currently use multiple disconnected systems:

-   POS
-   QR Payment
-   Inventory
-   CRM
-   Accounting
-   Loyalty
-   Marketing
-   Booking

This creates duplicated work and fragmented data.

ArusPay can unify these into one platform.

# Product Strategy

## Phase 1 -- Acquire Merchants (Free Forever)

Goal: - Remove barriers to adoption. - Onboard merchants quickly.

Modules: - POS Lite - QR Payment - Payment Link - Invoice & Quotation -
Digital Receipt - Inventory Lite - Customer CRM Lite - Analytics
Dashboard

Primary KPI: - Registered merchants - Active merchants - Daily
transactions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 2 -- Increase Daily Usage

Modules: - Loyalty - Membership - Voucher Engine - Supplier Management -
Purchase Orders - Expense Tracking - AI Business Insights

Goal: Merchants open ArusPay every day.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 3 -- Business Operations

Modules: - Staff Attendance - Payroll Integration - Multi-Branch -
Warehouse - Booking - Online Store - QR Ordering

Goal: Run the merchant's entire business.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 4 -- Financial Platform

Modules: - Merchant Wallet - Business Financing - Insurance - Supplier
Payments - Cash Advance

Goal: Transform ArusPay from a payment gateway into a financial
platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Merchant Journey

1.  Merchant signs up.
2.  Uses free POS.
3.  Accepts payments through ArusPay.
4.  Builds customer database.
5.  Uses inventory and analytics.
6.  Launches loyalty campaigns.
7.  Expands to multiple branches.
8.  Uses financing and premium services.

Each stage increases merchant value and retention.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Why This Strategy Works

We are not creating vendor lock-in.

We are creating **switching cost** through value.

Example after 2 years:

-   Customer database
-   Inventory history
-   Sales analytics
-   Loyalty points
-   Memberships
-   Digital receipts
-   Staff attendance
-   Booking history
-   Multi-branch reporting

A merchant can still leave, but doing so means rebuilding significant
operational history.
